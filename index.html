Information about Electric Vehicles

On EV–info.nl you can find an array of information about Electric Vehicles, mainly compared to Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) Vehicles, to help you decide if an Electric Vehicle (EV) is right for you!

Pros and cons of purchasing an EV compared to a gasoline powered car (ICE)

  • Pros
    1. Cheaper to fuel;
    2. More sustainable;
    3. Better future proofing;
    4. Less money to the fossil fuel industry.
  • Cons
    1. More expensive to purchase;
    2. Less range;
    3. Takes longer to fuel;
    4. Travelling is more difficult.

Why an EV?

An increasing amount of laws are being introduced to restrict or outright ban ICEs due to various environmental reasons, making EVs seem like they are the future. Even more so because EVs as technology are incredibly new compared to ICE vehicles. A car is already one of the most expensive single purchases and an EV is even more expensive than an ICE vehicle, but can an EV make up for that price difference? Is it cheaper to fuel an EV or a gasoline powered car? Find out here.

Assumptions

For all calculations on this website I assume the following:

  1. An original total car price between € 25.000 and € 45.000 (more on how to buy an EV cheaper here);
  2. A battery capacity between 40 and 60 kWh.
